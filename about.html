A Little About Me,

As a kid growing up in rural Vermont, my favorite thing to do was build. I would spend all day outside creating forts, bird houses, rock walls, you name it. When I grew up that desire only grew stronger.

When I went to college, I knew studying marketing alone wouldn’t keep my overactive brain satisfied so I decided to double major in media arts, allowing me to take classes in photo editing, design, and web development. While mastering those design skills and creating projects I realized I could get the same satisfaction on the computer as I did building things outside.

As a marketing manager for 5 years after college, I loved all the creative marketing and branding, but I couldn’t shake the designer itch. I finally took the leap in September 2018 to begin Bloc’s full-time User Experience Designer Program.

Through UX/UI Design, I found the type of career I had wanted since I was little. The opportunity to create beautiful designs for others has been tremendously fullfilling and I am so excited to face any new design challenge I am presented with.

When I'm not designing...

I love playing basketball, trying out new breweries, or hanging with Oscar, my golden doodle. On Sundays you can find me at Raymond James Stadium cheering on the Bucs and my Fiancé #77!
